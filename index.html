About Othello

A game of abstract strategy, Othello can only be played as a 2 player game. Players battle to finish the game with more of their own pieces on the board than their opponent.

Both players begin the game with two pieces on the board in the four center squares. Black must make the first move. A legal move consists of a piece being placed on the board that creates a straight line (vertical, horizontal or diagonal) made up of a piece of the player's color at either end and only pieces of the opponent's color in between. When a player achieves this line, they must complete the move by turning all of the opponent pieces in between their end pieces so that the line becomes entirely the player's color.

Players continue to take turns until they get to the end of the game and a winner is decided. The game is finished when there are no spaces left on the board or there are no more possible legal moves for either player.
